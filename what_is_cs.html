Carbon sequestration could be the solution to climate change entirly. But what is it? How does it work? To understand carbon sequestration a little better, here the link to a short video. Carbon Sequestration. Carbon sequestration besically is taking carbon dioxide (CO2) out of the air and storing it in things like geologic formations (geologic carbon sequesteration) or trees and plants (biologic carbon sequestration). Carbon sequestration is really important to many people. With this video link, you can get a better understanding of how important carbon sequesteration is to people who care.
